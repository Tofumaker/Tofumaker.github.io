BucketList

Bucketlist utlizes the IBM Watson API to analyze a piece of your own writing, or twitter, and suggests activities based on your personality and comfort level. The app learns more about an activity based on users' votes and makes smarter suggestions over time.

Go to app.

Tic or Toe

Tic or Toe is a 5x5 tic tac toe game with various difficulty settings. The entire project was developed from over a 3-day period. I was primarily responsible for the computer player's strategy algorithm. It should be impossible to beat on the most difficult setting... well at least no one has beaten it yet!

Go to app.
